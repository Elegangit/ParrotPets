Type of Parrots that make Adorable Pets and Best-Buddies

Cockatoo parrot seems to love that cup sound and Indian Ring Neck parrots are hilarious and adorable as well...

Parrots are omnivorous birds and can eat anything from fruits, wheat, seeds, to chillies (vegetables) and meat. Here is a video of parrots eating sunflower seeds.

All these parrots become an amazing companion pet and love their independence as well, so you can find them eat, drink water, shower, hang-out, play, learn and talk parrot or human just like a human child. One thing is for sure, all parrots need our love, attention, and support, so let's make them our best buddies by talking to them, feeding them healthy and not leaving them alone. Thanks all !
