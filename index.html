Cockatoo parrots are smart. They love to explore, especially sounds and mimic. This anti-cuptower cockatoo seems to love a cup sound.


All these parrots become an amazing companion and love their independence as they eat, drink water, shower, hang-out, play, learn and talk parrot or human. One thing is for sure, all parrots need our love, attention, and support, so let's make them our best buddies by talking to them, feeding them healthy and not leaving them alone or they'll fly away. ^_^
